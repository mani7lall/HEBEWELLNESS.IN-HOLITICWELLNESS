## The Biological Architecture

Unlike other biological systems that operate locally, the ECS is pervasive. It utilizes a vast network of cannabinoid receptors (CB1 and CB2) distributed throughout the central nervous system, peripheral organs, and immune cells.

- **Inflammatory Modulation:** Suppressing chronic cytokine storms.
- **Circadian Alignment:** Resetting the HPA axis for optimized sleep architecture.
