# Stop Buying Useless Vitamins

Walk into any health store, and you will see walls of colourful bottles promising extreme health, brain power, and longevity. The truth? 90% of them are expensive urine. 

Before diving into advanced peptides or exotic botanicals, you must secure the absolute baseline. Modern food is grown in depleted soil, and modern jobs keep us indoors. You cannot get these three nutrients from food or lifestyle alone.

## 1. Magnesium (The Relaxation Mineral)
Over 80% of modern adults are deficient in magnesium. It is responsible for over 300 different reactions in your body, most notably relaxing your muscles and calming your nervous system.

If you struggle to fall asleep, suffer from muscle cramps, or feel anxious, you likely need magnesium.

**What to buy:** Look for **Magnesium Glycinate** or **Magnesium Threonate**. Avoid Magnesium Oxide (it has terrible absorption and will just upset your stomach).
**When to take it:** 60 minutes before bed.

## 2. Vitamin D3 + K2 (The Sunshine Hormone)
Unless you work completely naked at the equator, you are deficient in Vitamin D. It is not actually a vitamin, but a powerful hormone that dictates your immune system, bone density, and mood.

**What to buy:** Always buy a liquid dropper that combines D3 with Vitamin K2. Vitamin D helps your body absorb calcium, but Vitamin K2 acts like a traffic cop, making sure that calcium goes into your bones, not into your arteries.
**When to take it:** In the morning with a meal containing some fat (like eggs).

## 3. High-Quality Omega-3 Fish Oil (The Brain Fuel)
The modern diet is packed with toxic, inflammatory Omega-6 seed oils (canola oil, soybean oil). To counteract this massive inflammation, you need high doses of clean Omega-3s (EPA and DHA). 

Omega-3s lubricate your joints, reduce brain fog, and protect your heart.

**What to buy:** Do not buy cheap fish oil at the grocery store—it is likely rancid. Buy a premium, wild-caught brand that explicitly lists the EPA and DHA amounts on the back (you want at least 1,000mg of EPA).
**When to take it:** With dinner.

> Master these three basics before you try to "bio-hack" your biology. They will provide 80% of the results.
