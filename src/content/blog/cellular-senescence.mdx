# The Zombie Cell Epidemic

Aging is not a single disease; it is an accumulation of micro-failures at the cellular level. The most destructive of these failures is **Cellular Senescence**.

As cells divide throughout your lifetime, their DNA slowly accumulates damage. Eventually, a healthy cell detects this damage and halts division to prevent becoming cancerous. While this is a necessary survival mechanism, these halted cells are supposed to undergo apoptosis (programmed cell death) and be cleared by the immune system.

However, as we age, our immune clearance falters. These damaged, non-dividing cells begin to linger in the tissues. These are Senescent Cells—often referred to as "Zombie Cells."

## The SASP Cascade

Zombie cells do not sit quietly. They secrete a highly toxic cocktail of inflammatory cytokines, proteases, and chemokines known as the **Senescence-Associated Secretory Phenotype (SASP)**.

SASP actively degrades surrounding healthy tissue, turning adjacent healthy cells into zombie cells. This localized inflammation is the fundamental root cause of almost every age-related disease: osteoarthritis, neurodegeneration, cardiovascular plaque, and metabolic syndrome.
