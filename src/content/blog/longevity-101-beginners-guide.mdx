# Where Do I Start?

We know the science of longevity can sound incredibly complicated. Words like *autophagy*, *mitochondrial bioenergetics*, and *mTOR inhibition* can be overwhelming. 

But you do not need to be a scientist to drastically improve your health span. This guide is designed to give you the exact, simple steps to start reversing your biological age today.

## Step 1: The Foundation of Sleep

Before you buy any supplements or try any complex diets, you must fix your sleep. Your body only repairs itself when it is entirely asleep.

**The Simple Rule:** Keep your bedroom pitch black and cold (around 65°F or 18°C). Avoid looking at phone or TV screens for 60 minutes before bed. If your mind is racing, a simple, high-quality CBD oil can help quiet the nervous system so you can drift off.

## Step 2: Eat Real Food, Less Often

You don't need a perfectly calculated macronutrient diet to start. Just follow two simple principles:

1. **Avoid Highly Processed Foods:** If it comes in a box with 30 ingredients you can't pronounce, don't eat it. Focus on single-ingredient foods: eggs, grass-fed meat, vegetables, and fruit.
2. **Give Your Digestion a Rest:** Try to eat all your meals within a 10 or 12-hour window (e.g., 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM). Fasting overnight gives your stomach a chance to rest and your cells a chance to clean out junk.

## Step 3: Move Your Body Every Day

You do not need to run a marathon. 
- Try to walk for 30 minutes every single day, ideally outside in the sun.
- Lift something heavy twice a week. Building just a little bit of muscle is the secret to keeping your metabolism fast and your bones strong as you age.

## Step 4: Manage Your Stress

Chronic stress is the ultimate ager. It produces cortisol, which breaks down muscle, stores fat, and ruins your sleep. 

Taking 5 minutes a day to sit quietly and take deep breaths tells your body that it is "safe." This simple act lowers inflammation and helps your body heal.

> "Longevity isn't about doing one complicated thing perfectly. It's about doing the simple things consistently."

Once you master these four steps, you can start exploring our more advanced protocols and peptide therapies. But build the foundation first!
