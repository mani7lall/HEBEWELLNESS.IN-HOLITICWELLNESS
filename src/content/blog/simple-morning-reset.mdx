# Win The Morning, Win The Day

How you spend the first 10 minutes after waking up dictates your energy levels, your hunger, and your mood for the entire rest of the day.

Most people wake up, immediately grab their phone to check emails, and drink a massive cup of coffee on an empty stomach. This spikes anxiety, ruins your natural cortisol rhythm, and guarantees a massive energy crash by 2:00 PM.

Here is the simple, 10-minute Hebe Morning Reset.

## Minute 1-2: Hydration First

Before you drink coffee, your body desperately needs water. You just went 8 hours without drinking!

**The Action:** Drink a large glass of water immediately upon waking. Add a pinch of sea salt or a squeeze of lemon to replenish the electrolytes you lost overnight. This alone will wake up your brain faster than caffeine.

## Minute 3-7: Sunlight 

Your internal clock (your circadian rhythm) is entirely controlled by light. If you stay inside a dark house all morning, your brain thinks it is still nighttime and you will feel groggy all day.

**The Action:** Step outside. You need natural sunlight hitting your eyes (do not look directly at the sun, just look outside). Even if it is cloudy, the natural light is 100x stronger than your indoor lightbulbs. This stops the production of melatonin (the sleep hormone) and tells your body to produce energy.

## Minute 8-10: Gentle Movement

You don't need to do a full workout, but you do need to increase your blood flow to clear out stiffness.

**The Action:** Do 2 minutes of light stretching or yoga. Reach for your toes, twist your torso, and take 10 deep breaths. 

## What About Coffee?

Wait at least 60 to 90 minutes after waking up before you drink caffeine. 

When you first wake up, your body naturally produces cortisol to get you going. If you drink coffee immediately, you override this natural process. By waiting 90 minutes, you allow your natural energy to peak, and the coffee will carry you through the entire afternoon without a crash.

> "A powerful day is built on the foundation of a deliberate morning. Protect your first 10 minutes."
