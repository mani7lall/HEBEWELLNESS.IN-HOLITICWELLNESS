## Translating the Doshas into Endocrinology

The Ayurvedic system classifies human physiology into three primary bio-energetic forces, or *Doshas*: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha. While these terms sound unscientific to the Western ear, they correlate fiercely with modern human endocrinology and metabolic phenotypes.

* **Vata (Air/Space):** Correlates with the central nervous system, rapid neurotransmitter firing, and catabolic (tissue-breaking) metabolism. Vata imbalance mirrors chronic sympathetic overdrive (high cortisol/adrenaline).
* **Pitta (Fire/Water):** Correlates with the endocrine system, enzymatic digestion, and metabolic heat. Pitta imbalance equates to systemic inflammation, heavily marked by high C-reactive protein (CRP) levels.
* **Kapha (Earth/Water):** Correlates with structural integrity, immune function, and anabolic growth. Kapha stagnation mirrors insulin resistance and lymphatic congestion.
