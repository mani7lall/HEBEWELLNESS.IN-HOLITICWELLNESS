# The Irrelevance of '8 Hours'

Modern wellness is obsessed with duration: "Get 8 hours of sleep." This is a profoundly flawed metric. If you spend 8 hours in light sleep (N1 and N2 stages) interrupted by micro-wakes from cortisol spikes or alcohol rebound, you achieve zero biological repair.

The only metric that matters is **Sleep Architecture**: the precise percentage distribution of Deep Sleep (Slow-Wave Sleep) and REM (Rapid Eye Movement).

## Slow-Wave Sleep (Delta Phase)

Deep sleep is characterized by massive, slow Delta brainwaves (0.5 to 4 Hz). During this exact phase, three extreme biological events occur:
1. **Human Growth Hormone (HGH) Pulse:** 70% of your daily HGH is released during the first cycle of deep sleep. This repairs torn muscle fibers, organs, and bone density.
2. **Glymphatic Clearance:** The brain literally shrinks by up to 20%, allowing cerebrospinal fluid to wash through the neural tissue, clearing out the toxic amyloid-beta plaques associated with Alzheimer's.
3. **Memory Consolidation:** Short-term motor skills and facts are physically hard-coded into the cortex.

If your core body temperature does not drop by 1°C to 2°C, or if your heart rate remains elevated from late-night food digestion, the brain refuses to enter Delta phase.
